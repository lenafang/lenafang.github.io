Selected Projects

Problems I've solved

Moomoo · Fintech KPI Design
Redefining Growth KPI for Moomoo US
Situation Growth KPIs focused on PC volume and total AUM drove teams toward short-term, low-quality acquisition — resulting in poor retention and low ROI.
Approach Diagnosed KPI-driven behavior across BD and channels. Redesigned framework around core principles (Focused, Achievable, Trackable) — shifted primary KPI from PCs to effective traders with meaningful early activity, added CAC and quarter-end AUM retention, weighted KPIs by channel.
↑ Impact Measurable ARPU improvement among new acquired users · shifted acquisition mix toward higher-retention cohorts
Moomoo · Fintech User Activation
Diagnosing the First-Trade Activation Gap
Situation Moomoo's revenue depends on trading activity. Despite acquisition spend, a significant portion of paid users never placed a first trade — wasted CAC, zero trading revenue.
Approach Identified the critical activation window from historical data. Built layered behavioral analysis: content engagement (feed, community, courses) → sharing behavior → active browsing and watchlist additions. Translated into an operational playbook.
↑ Impact Delivered a data-backed activation playbook — what to surface, when, and to whom, to move users toward first trade
Walmart · Big Retail Retention Analytics
Protecting Sam's Club Membership Revenue Before It Churned
Situation Rapid new-store expansion meant first-year members made up a large share of the base. This cohort had historically low renewal rates — without early intervention, a wave of non-renewals would erode membership and revenue.
Approach Modeled key renewal drivers from historical data. Designed a phased intervention playbook: (0–30 days) onboarding — welcome kits, offline visit, social follow; (mid-period) increase purchase frequency, category breadth, online engagement; (near renewal) drive offline store visit, activate cashier touchpoint. Built monitoring dashboard.
↑ Impact Delivered a "what to do, when" intervention playbook + dashboard — gave the business a concrete, data-driven path to protect first-year renewal rates
